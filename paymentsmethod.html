Payment Via BSP (SMS banking)

To make a payment via BSP SMS banking, please follow these steps:

  1. Dial *131#.
  2. Select "Funds Transfer" from the menu.
  3. Choose "Other BSP accounts"
  4. Enter the amount you wish to send.
  5. And send it to this ACC# 7027972996:
  6. Do take a screenshot of the transaction and send it to us via the contact form below.
  7. Confirm the payment details and submit.
